About the Meetup

Bitcoinology is a monthly event dedicated to exploring the many aspects of Bitcoin. Our mission is to educate, inform, and connect enthusiasts and newcomers alike.

Support us:

Hosting in London is not cheap. The meetup is free, but if you find it useful or appreciate our efforts, we would appreciate any contributions you could make to help us continue these meetups.

As a Bitcoin only meetup, we accept contributions via bitcoin only. Visit our official Geyser page if you would like to contribute.

Who are we?

Bitcoinology is organised and hosted by three volunteers:

  • Antoine: Bitcoin Core contributor, Security expert and Bitcoin Lightning developer.
  • Dee: A software developer by day, bitcoin enthusiast by night.
  • Ramon: Bitcoin enthusiast, tinkerer and not a wizard.
